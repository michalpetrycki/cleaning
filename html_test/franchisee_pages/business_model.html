The Diamond Home Support Cleaning Franchise Business Model

With a Diamond Home Support cleaning franchise business you do not do any cleaning and you do not need to employ anyone either. A lot of people find that a bit strange! How on earth can you provide a cleaning service where no-one does the cleaning?!

Our franchisees are, if you like, the middle man. They find self-employed workers who can provide the services we offer (cleaning, ironing, home help, gardening etc…) and then match them with clients who are looking for someone to provide those services for them. From this they earn around £3 per hour. The cleaning franchise earning opportunity with our business model is tried and tested and with few overheads it really is the ideal management franchise that can be run from home.

In the early days we recommend the business is run from home and all you really need is a computer, a phone and a little storage space for leaflets. You don’t even need any equipment or cleaning materials as your workers will be using the equipment and materials of clients (this avoids problems with allergies and preferences for certain smells etc…). Our cleaning franchise business can be run in your spare time if you wish and can grow at the rate you want it to. If you want to build a small business to run alongside your current employment then this is ideal. It’s also ideal for those wishing to build a secure part or full-time income. You can pretty much take this business anywhere you wish.

For just £2,495 we provide everything you need to get started in our cleaning franchise package.
