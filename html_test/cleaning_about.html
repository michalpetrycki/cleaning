About Us

Diamond Home Support is a professional domestic cleaning and home support business. We are a rapidly growing organisation with offices throughout the UK. Our local offices provide a variety of services including: domestic cleaning, ironing, shopping, gardening and home help type services. We understand that what you want from a domestic service provider is reliable, professional and trustworthy workers. All of our workers are vetted and insured by us personally to ensure only the best service is delivered to our customers.

In the main, if you choose us, you will have the same worker every week or every fortnight. That way they quickly understand your requirements and can offer a regular service made to meet your personal needs. We will also endeavour to provide an uninterrupted service even if your normal worker is sick or on holiday. Should you ever be unhappy with a worker we provide we will replace them without question.

All of our offices offer cleaning and ironing services. Some also offer gardening and extra support for the elderly. For further information on what we do in your area please visit your relevant local office page.

If you would like to join us and open a local office in your area find out about our cleaning franchise opportunity. Find out if we offer a domestic cleaning service in your area by searching our list of local offices.
