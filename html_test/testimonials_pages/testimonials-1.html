“The main benefit of being a Diamond Home Support Franchisee is being your own boss and knowing that everything you put into the business benefits you as opposed to somebody else’s business!”

Astrid and Ian run our Callington and Tavistock local offices. They have been with the franchise since May 2012 and are really enjoying the experience.

“We like the predictability of income from month to month.”

Astrid and Ian began initially in Callington and quickly realised the potential of the business which resulted in them expanding into Tavistock and beyond just 3 months into the business.

Ian told us “We saw this as a way of taking control of Astrid’s working experience having only previously worked for other organisations and having had an unhappy experience working in the public sector.”

Ian is a full time serving police officer, but is also active within the business and helps out by delivering leaflets and takes care of the accounting side.

“Having identified the area in which we wanted to operate and done research about the various franchise options Diamond Home Support, with its low initial fee and clear fixed monthly franchise payment, was very attractive relative to other comparable business models.”

“The Operations Manual is comprehensive and clear and as neither of us had set up a business before we found it enormously helpful in terms of suggested contacts and advertising routes as well as a suggested timetable for the first couple of months.”

“The leaflets are professional and admired by clients who receive them. Support by the franchisor has without exception been prompt and efficient.”

Astrid and Ian are looking forward to the prospects of continuing to grow their Diamond Home Support business because, in their words, “it really works!”.
