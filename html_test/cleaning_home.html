Do You Need Cleaning, Home Help or Gardening?

Are you finding it difficult to cope?

Would you benefit from the services of professional domestic support? Diamond Home Support offers domestic cleaning, ironing, home help services and gardening by vetted and insured workers at affordable rates.

We offer a range of services across much of the UK. Our cleaners are reliable and experienced professionals. We offer both regular and one-off cleaning. If you have a regular service from us then you will have the same cleaner every visit so that they get to know you and your requirements.

Some of our offices also provide home help services such as: shopping, laundry, bed making, companionship and escorting to appointments. In some areas we can also provide gardening such as grass cutting, weeding and pruning.

To find out what we can do for you please contact your nearest local office.
